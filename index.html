Frequently asked questions

What is email marketing for small business?

Email marketing for small business is sending targeted, timely messages to your customer list to drive repeat purchases, website visits, and in-store traffic. It returns an average of $36 for every $1 spent — the highest ROI of any marketing channel available to small businesses.

What is SMS marketing and why does it work?

SMS marketing uses text messages to reach customers directly on their phones. Texts have a 98% open rate and are read within 3 minutes — making SMS marketing the fastest channel for time-sensitive promotions, new arrivals, and driving same-day foot traffic or online visits.

How is The Send Desk different from large marketing agencies?

Large agencies charge $5,000–$25,000 per month and assign junior staff to most accounts. The Send Desk gives you one experienced senior strategist, right-sized pricing, and direct communication — doing the same quality work at a fraction of the cost. We work with small businesses, not around them.

How do I build an email and SMS list from scratch?

We build your list-building strategy using every touchpoint your business already has: point of sale, your website, social media, events, and your physical space. We set up the capture points, train your team, and grow a list of real interested customers before sending a single campaign.

How often will you email or text my customers?

Never more than they want to hear from you. We build a strategic calendar based on your business type, seasonality, and customer behavior — not a rigid schedule. Automated sequences send when triggered by a customer action. Campaign sends are planned around real business moments, not arbitrary frequency.

What kinds of small businesses do you work with?

We work with restaurants, breweries, taprooms, boutiques, salons, spas, fitness studios, pet services, home service businesses, gift shops, wellness practices, and creative studios — any independent small business with customers worth keeping and a list worth using.

What is a win-back campaign?

A win-back campaign is an automated email or SMS message sent to customers who have gone quiet — no visit, no purchase, no engagement. We identify who they are, choose the right channel and message, and send it at the moment they're most likely to respond. Most businesses never try this. The ones that do are surprised how many come back.
